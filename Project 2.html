Conclusions¶

It can be concluded from the graphics, that the people who attend the doctor have the closest appointments. No relationship found between Gender and No show to appointments. Anyway, women have more medical appointments. Although the average Age is similar in No show and Show appointments, it can be seen from the graphics that younger and older people are the main ones to go to medical appointments mostly.
